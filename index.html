AxBuss Technologies Inc. is a Web based Start Up company created in 2010 in Barquisimeto, Venezuela. We are a dedicated team of web savvy entrewebneurs from all walks of life with a variety of talents (Languages and Communications, Net Architecture, Online Project Design and Development, International Studies, Networking, Applied Mathematics). These talents create a high synergy approach that we bring to bear when it comes down to Webusiness Project Development.

We take pride in our teamwork, flexibility and work ethics. Our business proposal includes a fresh vision of where the Online Marketing Industry is going and how factors such as the new generation of tablet and mobile applications with better content accessibility, software/hardware improvements influence our customer's online experience. Our goal is to make this experience as productive as possible for all the parties involved.
